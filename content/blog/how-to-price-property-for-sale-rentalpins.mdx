Pricing is the fastest lever sellers control. On RentalPins Buy, buyers compare your pin against neighbours on the map — overpricing pushes you off shortlists before anyone calls.

## Start with nearby comps

Before you publish, search the [sale map](/buy/search) around your society. Note BHK, floor, furnishing, and asking totals for similar units within 2–3 km.

## Use total price clarity

State whether your price includes parking, fixtures, or transfer charges. Ambiguity slows WhatsApp threads and erodes trust.

## Refresh if stale

If enquiries drop after two weeks, revisit price or photos. Buyers assume stale listings have title issues.
