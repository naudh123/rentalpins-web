## Safety checklist for parents and students

1. **Visit in daylight** — inspect street lighting and lane access
2. **Confirm warden or caretaker** — who handles daily issues?
3. **Guest and curfew rules** — written clarity on WhatsApp before deposit
4. **Bathroom sharing ratio** — overcrowded PGs show stress quickly
5. **Food quality** — taste mess food if meals are included
6. **Map pin accuracy** — building should match RentalPins location
