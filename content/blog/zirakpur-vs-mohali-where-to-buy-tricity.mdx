Zirakpur and Mohali sit on opposite sides of the buyer decision: established Mohali phases versus Zirakpur's VIP Road and Dhakoli growth belts. Neither is universally "better" — your commute anchor and budget band decide.

## Mohali buyer profile

Families and professionals anchored to IT Park, Aerocity, or Chandigarh sectors often shortlist [Mohali buy hub](/buy/mohali) areas first — Phase 7, Sector 70, Aerocity, and New Chandigarh corridors.

## Zirakpur buyer profile

Buyers prioritising entry price or airport-corridor rental yield often start on the [Zirakpur buy map](/buy/zirakpur) — especially VIP Road and Dhakoli micro-markets.
