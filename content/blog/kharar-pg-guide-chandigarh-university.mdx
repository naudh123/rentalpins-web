Chandigarh University students landing in Kharar face the same question every admission season: which PG is actually walkable, includes decent meals, and will not drain your budget before classes start?

Kharar is one of the most affordable belts in Chandigarh Tricity — but not every listing labelled "near CU" is honest about distance. Map-led search beats scrolling generic classifieds because you see where the pin sits before you visit.

## Best areas for CU students in Kharar

### Kharar town core

Dense PG and room inventory with markets, pharmacies, and daily needs nearby. Best if you want maximum amenity access on a budget and do not mind slightly busier streets.

### Kharar–Landran road

The highest-turnover student corridor — hostels and PG clusters serving CU and nearby institutes. Compare food quality and room ventilation, not just headline rent.

### Sunny Enclave and Gillco Valley

Popular when two to four friends want a **shared flat** instead of PG rules. Slightly higher rent per unit, but more privacy and space if you split costs.

## PG vs shared flat near CU

### When PG makes sense

- First-year students who want meals and fixed monthly costs
- Parents who prefer managed accommodation with curfew and security
- Short stays where furnishing a flat is not practical

### When a shared flat wins

- Groups of friends with stable roommate agreements
- Students who cook independently and want fewer house rules
- Second year onwards when you know your commute and budget clearly

Run both filters on the same map before deciding — the cheapest PG is not always cheapest once you add food, laundry, and Wi‑Fi top-ups.

## What to ask owners before visiting

1. **Exact walk or ride time to CU gates** — not just "near campus"
2. **Food plan** — veg/non-veg, meal timings, and whether snacks are included
3. **Room sharing** — single, double, or triple occupancy
4. **AC and electricity** — included or metered separately
5. **Deposit and notice period** — standard in Kharar is often one to two months' rent
6. **Who manages daily issues** — owner, caretaker, or warden

Message owners through RentalPins after saving three to five pins — serious inquiries with move-in dates get faster responses during peak season.

## Viewing checklist for Kharar PG

- Visit in **daylight** first — check street lighting and access paths
- Inspect **bathroom cleanliness** and water pressure
- Ask about **laundry** — in-house, weekly pickup, or self-service
- Confirm **guest and curfew rules** if you expect occasional family visits
- Compare **ventilation and sunlight** — basement rooms look cheap until monsoon
