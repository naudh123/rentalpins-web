Owner-direct discovery on [RentalPins Buy](/buy/search) removes broker search friction — it does not replace legal due diligence. Use this checklist before token in Mohali, Kharar, Zirakpur, or Panchkula.

## Title and seller authority

Confirm who is signing the agreement — owner, power-of-attorney holder, or builder representative for under-construction inventory. Match listing contact with sale documents.

## Society and municipal approvals

For apartments, ask about society transfer norms, outstanding maintenance, and parking allotment. For plots, check zoning and building plan approvals with local authorities.

## Financial outgoings

Capture maintenance, transfer charges, property tax, and any society renovation fund demands in writing before you negotiate final price.

## Physical inspection

Visit at least twice — weekday evening for traffic noise, weekend for society activity. Compare the map pin location with the actual gate you will use daily.
