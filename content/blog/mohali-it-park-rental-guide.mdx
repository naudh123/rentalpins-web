## Phase-wise search for IT Park commuters

Mohali is not one uniform market. Headline rent differs sharply across phases even when listings use similar titles.

### Premium phases — Phase 7, 9, 11, Aerocity

Established societies, stronger amenity stacks, and steady demand from IT professionals and corporate relocations. Furnished 1 BHK and 2 BHK units move fastest at hiring season peaks.

### Value phases — Phase 3B2, Phase 5

More affordable entry points with reasonable IT Park connectivity. Good for young professionals optimizing budget while keeping commute under control.

### Airport Road and Phase 8 belts

Active **PG and hostel** inventory for students and short-term professionals. Check food plans, curfew rules, and AC charges before visiting.

## Move-in checklist for Mohali tenants

1. Confirm rent includes **maintenance, parking, and power backup**
2. Ask about **visitor, pet, and lift policies** in societies
3. Photograph existing damage at handover — share date-stamped images on WhatsApp
4. Clarify **deposit return** and painting charges before paying token
5. Sort shortlist by **peak-hour commute**, not lowest headline rent alone

Respond quickly when owners reply — Mohali tenants often finalize one of three shortlisted pins within a week during peak season.
