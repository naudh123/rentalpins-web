A good sale listing is not the longest description — it is the clearest one. Buyers shortlist on map pins first, then open detail for photos, price, and society context. This checklist helps Mohali and Tricity sellers publish once and answer better inquiries.

## Before you open List for Sale

Gather:

- **6–12 clear photos** — living area, kitchen, bathrooms, balcony, society gate if allowed  
- **Facts sheet** — BHK, super area vs carpet if known, floor, facing, parking  
- **Asking price** — your target; be ready to explain inclusions  
- **Possession timeline** — ready, tenant-occupied, or under construction  
- **Document pack** (for serious buyers) — sale deed copy, society NOC norms, ID proof

## Map pin accuracy matters

Place the pin at the **building or plot entrance**, not a random sector label. Buyers use distance to offices, colleges, and highways when shortlisting. Wrong pins waste everyone’s time and reduce trust.

## Write descriptions buyers actually scan

Lead with:

1. Property type and society/plot name  
2. BHK and floor  
3. Asking price and negotiability band  
4. One line on why the location works (commute anchor)  
5. What is included — parking, appliances, registry status

Avoid ALL CAPS price drops and duplicate broker copy pasted from other boards.

## Handle inquiries without chaos

- Reply with viewing slots in batches — e.g. Saturday 10–1  
- Confirm who will show the flat (owner, family, caretaker)  
- Share the same facts you published — surprises on visit kill deals  
- Never share OTPs or full bank details in chat
