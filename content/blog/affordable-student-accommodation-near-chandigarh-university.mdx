## Hidden costs to ask before token

- Electricity (metered or included?)
- AC charges in summer
- Laundry — in-house or paid per load?
- Wi‑Fi speed and data caps
- Guest and curfew penalties
- Deposit refund rules

## PG vs flat on a budget

- **Solo first-year student:** PG with meals often wins on predictable monthly cost.
- **Group of friends:** Shared flat can beat PG per person — see [PG vs flat guide](/blog/pg-vs-flat-near-chandigarh-university).
