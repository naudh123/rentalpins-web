Commercial buyers need footfall, visibility, and compliant use — not just square footage. RentalPins Buy lists owner-direct shops, showrooms, offices, and warehouses in Tricity.

## Filter the buy map

Open [commercial property for sale](/commercial-property-for-sale) or the [sale map](/buy/search) and use commercial sub-type chips — Shops, Showroom, Office Space, Warehouse.

## Verify use permissions

Residential conversions marketed as commercial are a common trap. Ask for approved building use and society or municipal norms in writing.

## Compare yield if investing

If rental income matters, ask occupied units for current rent, lease term, and deposit — then compare yield against residential alternatives in the same budget.
