**Flats near Chandigarh University** suit student groups who outgrow PG rules and want to cook, split rent, and control their own schedule — especially from second year onward.

## Best flat belts for CU students

### Kharar town

Mix of independent floors and small apartments. Good when your group wants city access and shorter commute.

### Sunny Enclave

Quieter residential streets; popular for **2–3 BHK** batchmate groups. Slightly higher rent, better privacy.

### Gillco Valley

Society-style flats; confirm **student occupancy** and guest rules with owners before signing.

Browse flats on the [Kharar rentals map](https://www.rentalpins.com/rentals/in/chandigarh/kharar).

## Group flat checklist

Before signing near CU:

1. **Agree rent split** in writing among roommates
2. **Confirm owner allows** four student tenants if applicable
3. **Utilities** — electricity, water, Wi‑Fi split method
4. **Furnishing** — what is included vs what you buy
5. **Deposit and notice** — standard is often one to two months' rent
6. **Visit in daylight** — check water pressure, ventilation, society access

## Flat vs PG reminder

First-year solo students often stay in PG. Groups with stable roommate agreements frequently shift to flats — see [PG vs flat near CU](/blog/pg-vs-flat-near-chandigarh-university).
