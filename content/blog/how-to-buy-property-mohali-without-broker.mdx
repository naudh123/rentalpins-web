Buying in Mohali is less about finding *a* flat and more about comparing the right micro-market for your commute, society, and resale liquidity. RentalPins Buy helps you start with owner-direct map listings — then validate like a careful buyer, not a rushed bidder.

## Start on the Mohali buy map

Open [RentalPins Buy](/buy/search) and pan to Mohali. Filter by budget and property type, then shortlist pins in phases or sectors that match your daily anchor — IT Park, Aerocity, Chandigarh commute, or New Chandigarh growth belts.

Owner-direct listings mean you message sellers from listing detail. That does not remove the need for legal due diligence — it removes broker search friction while you compare options.

## Compare sectors before you fall in love with one photo

Mohali buyers often cross-shop:

- **Established phases** (Phase 7, 11, Sector 70) for mature societies and clearer resale comps  
- **Aerocity / airport belt** for newer inventory and rental demand from professionals  
- **New Chandigarh corridors** for plotted and apartment projects — see our [New Chandigarh investment guide](/buy/new-chandigarh-investment-guide)

Use map distance, not vague “near Phase X” titles. Two listings can share a headline and differ sharply in daily drive time.

## Buyer checklist before token

1. **Seller identity** — who holds keys, is it owner or authorised representative?  
2. **Title & society docs** — sale deed chain, occupancy/possession where relevant, society NOC norms  
3. **Outgoings** — maintenance, parking, transfer charges, any pending dues  
4. **Physical visit** — daylight inspection, society access, water pressure, society rules  
5. **Written terms on WhatsApp** — price, inclusions, timeline, refund if deal fails

RentalPins does not guarantee title or possession — you confirm those directly with the seller and your advisor.
